Thank you for visiting, we hope you'll enjoy your stay.

We are a rural parish church in the heart of Sidlow at Reigate, growing in love for our God and for each other, and we'd love to meet you. We are part of the Diocese of Southwark and the Church of England.

If you're new to the area, we'd love to get to know you; why not speak to some of the welcome team during the service who will gladly help you with any questions you may have. Alternatively you can connect with us via our contact page.
